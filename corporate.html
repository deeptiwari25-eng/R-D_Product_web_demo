The Problem

Messy Worker Management

Our Fix: BuildEase

Smart Attendance & Matching

The Problem

Material Chaos & Theft

Work stops completely because someone forgot to order cement. Up to 15% of materials get wasted or stolen from the site with no tracking.

Our Fix: BuildEase

Live Tracking & Supplier Market
